Transition Squad

 

From January 2013, the Transition squad will be separated into two squads, Transition A and Transition B squad.  Please see the new list of squad members to determine which squad you are in and see below for your associated training times.

 

Transition B – a teaching session, learning the building blocks of training including turns, starts, finishes, legal strokes, how to follow written instructions and use the pace clock and learning a variety of training drills. Participation in club championships and time trials is also expected. 


Transition A – a training session where swimmers will be able to train with minimal instruction, and be competent in all of the above Transition B objectives.
