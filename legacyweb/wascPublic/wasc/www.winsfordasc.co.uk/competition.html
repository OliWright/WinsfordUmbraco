This has news for juggernaut squad members (which used to be Comp A and B)
 


 

Head Coach, Dan and swimmer
I am sure all swimmers will have dreams and goals for the new season, now you have the chance to discuss them with me your coach!

With the new season fast approaching I would like to sit down with every swimmer in Juggernaut individually to discuss last season’s training and competition results and to discuss the new season’s targets and what we have to do to achieve them.

This meeting is a two way process between swimmer and coach and I hope it will motivate all who attend to achieve great things for 2014-2015. Please come to the meeting with ideas on what was good about last season and what can be improved, in terms of training and performance. Also come to the meeting with some targets for the new season and what you will need to do to achieve these.

The meeting will only be for 10 minutes sorry! We have over 40 swimmers in Juggernaut and even though I would like to talk with you longer it would take many weeks to get through everyone! But you will all have 10 minutes of just you and me to discuss everything about you! So please take up the opportunity and fill in your name on the sheet below. Meetings are not compulsory so if you feel you would prefer not to have one then that is fine, not a problem.

Please remember to arrive at least 5 minutes early so you are not late for your meeting, I will stick to the time scale exactly. All meetings will take place at Winsford Lifestyle centre in the public cafe area. It is for swimmer and coach only, no parents sorry! Parents are more than welcome to wait in the foyer/cafe area.

The sheet will be left in the coaches’ room on the table where the computer is for you to fill in your preferred time slot, the sheet will be out before every squad training session starting Monday 25th August up to and including Tuesday 2nd September. First come first served.

I Hope all swimmers can make it because I want each and every one of you to believe and achieve. Meetings will take place at date and time on form.

Platinum, Gold and Silver swimmers will have a group meeting, date TBC.

Thanks Dan.
