Swimming lessons

Lessons schedule June 2015)
Schedule June 2015

The swimming club lessons have now finished for the summer holidays.  We will be running crash course over the holidays.  If you are interested please contact our Facebook page for more details and to book a place.

From September Brio leisure will be running all stage 1 - 5 lessons.  If you are in stage 5 currently you will transfer into the clubs stage 6 lesson.  If you are currently in stages 1 - 4 then you will need to contact Winsford reception to complete a Learn2 swim application form to join their lessons.  There should be no change to the lessons we are delivering; with the same teachers, helpers, day and time of lessons etc.  If you have any queries then please speak to Michael.  He will be at the pool on Sunday between 5-7.30 all summer.


We would like to ask all parents to please take note of the day, time, and lane of your child's lesson from the lessons schedule. Lane 1 is the nearest lane to the changing rooms and Lane 6 is by the window (layout below). Please ensure children arrive on poolside 5 minutes before the start of their lesson, so that lessons can start promptly and registers taken.

From 1st September, we have a new way in which parents can recieve feedback regarding their childrens progress in their lesson. Please email feedbackwasc@gmail.com for all information regarding your child progress. We will respond within 7-10 days. This allows us enough time

to consult with your child's teacher, so that a clear picture of your child's progress can be given. Initially, it, may take a little longer than 10 days if there is a huge demand, so please be patient. This is our preferred method of feedback. Discussing your child's progress after their lesson with the teacher directly is not ideal, as they may be getting ready to teach another class. If you have any queries please email vandbkeeling@btinternet.com
