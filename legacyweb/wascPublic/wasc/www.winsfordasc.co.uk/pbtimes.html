PB lists which include conversions show the faster of your actual time or your converted time for a given distance.

If you intend to use converted times from the attached files for entries at regional level, we recommend that you double-check the conversion using either the tables available on the ASA website or www.pullbuoy.co.uk. It is your responsibility to ensure that your times are accurately converted.

Some meets will accept times achieved at time trials and league galas whilst others will only consider times from licensed meets.

Some meets will only consider times achieved within the last 10-12 months. Please check the meet conditions before entering.
